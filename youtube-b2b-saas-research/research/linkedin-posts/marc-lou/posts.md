I built Managed Proxy for DataFa.st

AdBlockers screw up 30% of analytics data, so if you want accuracy, you need to proxy analytics events through your own domain.

Managed Proxy lets you do this without coding.

1. Add your proxy subdomain to DataFast like a(.)domain(.)com (don't use analytics-related subdomain, adblockers flag that too).
2. Add a CNAME record to your DNS.
3. Update the <script /> to point to your subdomain instead of DataFast.
4. 🎉

Available for free to all users ❤️

Get started here: https://lnkd.in/gzUx7CbJ

PS. If you're already proxying the DataFast script with the other methods, you don't need Managed Proxy. This is just a simpler alternative for no-code people.
